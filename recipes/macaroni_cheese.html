MAcaroni and cheese.

Macaroni and cheese recipe. The only serious recipe in this project. It's one of my favorite meals actually. Not that dense from a caloric pov if u ask me(164kcal per 100g), but it isn't something healthy either.

Step 1: Boil Macaroni

Bring a pot of water to a boil. Cook elbow macaroni until al dente, about 8 minutes.
Tip: For a thicker mac and cheese, double the amount of macaroni.
