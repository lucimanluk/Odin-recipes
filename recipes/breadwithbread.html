Bread with bread

That's right. Bread with bread. Or as we usually call it, bread sandwich.

How to make a bread sandwich:

Step 1:take bread

Take 3 or more bread slices.

Step 2:put bread on top of bread

Put 2 bread slices together.

Step 3:put more bread

Put 1 or more bread slice(s) between the 2 existing slices and that's it. Enjoy.
