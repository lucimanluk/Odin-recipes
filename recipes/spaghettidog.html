Spaghettidog.

Oh yeah. Spaghettidog. You haven't heard of this, have you? It's basically a hotdog with spaghetti.

Step 1:take a sausage

Take your sausage ( ͡° ͜ʖ ͡°) and put it on the table.

Step 2:get some spaghetti

Get some spaghetti from your cupboard, preferably 3 to 4 pieces.

Step 3:impale the sausage

Take your spaghetti and impale the sausage with it.

Step 4:boil the sausage

Boil the sausage for 5-10 min. That's it. Enjoy ( ͡° ͜ʖ ͡°).
